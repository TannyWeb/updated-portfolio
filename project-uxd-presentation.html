CEX UXD Presentation

- User Experience Design Presentation -

Description

The task was to apply a variety of user experience design techniques to evaluate and critique selected web-based interfaces, making recommendations for improvements and adjustments.

The second part of my final year UXD project involved me creating a 15 min presentation evaluating the CEX website against common UX techniques such as an expert review, guerrilla testing and eye tracking software.

I chose the website CEX to review as it was an extremely popular second hand shop which many people including myself have used and had huge amounts of trouble trying to purchase any sort of product from there.

After completing the presentation, I have learnt how to integrate vital user experience design tools and techniques to assess how a website can be improved and modernised.
